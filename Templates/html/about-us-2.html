ABOUT US

Nautical Marine is a Global Full-Spectrum Maritime Services Company.

Established in 1992, Nautical Marine Management has ascended to international prominence within the maritime industry. Originating as a modest team in a rented office in India's commercial hub, we have evolved into a comprehensive crew and ship management powerhouse. Today, our global presence spans 14 countries, boasting 1,150 dedicated shore-based professionals and a cadre of 25,000 active seafarersâ€”a testament to our resilience and unwavering client focus in a dynamic market.

Our enduring success is grounded in our commitment to understanding shipowners' needs and making them our central concern. We craft tailor-made solutions, harmonizing cost-efficiency and quality through established procedures, adaptive management systems, and a wealth of industry insight. Our extensive experience empowers us to meticulously plan, integrate, and deliver optimal results for our clients.

Over the past three decades, our service portfolio has expanded to encompass various ship management facets, such as crew management, technical ship management, new building supervision, finance and administration, crew training, safety, and offshore crewing. Within our full management department, we oversee a diverse fleet, including container ships, bulk carriers, multipurpose vessels, tankers, and specialized craft. At Nautical Marine Management, our enduring legacy is one of unwavering excellence in the maritime realm.
